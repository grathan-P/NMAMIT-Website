Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT),Ntte, established in 1986 and recognised by the AII. India counsil for Technical education,New Delhi, is now a constitution college of Nitte (Deemed to be university). Mangaluru, since June 2022. Rank band 151-200 in the national institutional Ranking frame work (NIRF) 2024 by Ministry of Education , Government of India, the college has been place under 'platinum' category of having high industry linkages by AICTE-CIC survey of Idustry-linked technical-Institution 2020. NMAMIT, off-campus center DU located at Nitte in karkal taluk, has active collabration with several international universities and organization for faculty and student exchange, research, intership & placement

The institution offers UG Engineering programmes in fifteen supliness, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Computer Science & Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science), Computer Science & Engineering (IoT & Cyber Security), Computer Science & Engineering (AI & DS), Computer Science & Engineering (AI & IOT), Information Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology, Industrial Engineering & Management and Aeronautical Engineering. The institution also offers PG programmes in Computer Science & Engineering, VLSI Design & Embedded Systems, Power Electronics, Structural Engineering, Thermal Engineering and MBA. The institution has research centers in all the departments offering Ph.D. programmes.
